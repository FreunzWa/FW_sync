###### Shock
    4 categories
        Distributive shock (tank too big)
            Sepsis, Neurogenic, Anaphylactic
        Obstructive Shock  (blockage to outlet)
            Massive PE
            Tension Pneumo
            Cardiac Tamponadde
        Hypovolaemic shock  (not enough water in tank)
            Haemorrhage
        Cardiogenic shock (heart not working)
            AMI
            Myocarditis
