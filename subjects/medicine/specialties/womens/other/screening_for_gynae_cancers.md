###### Screening for gynaecology cancers

# Cervical cancer
- risk factors
    + DES (diethylstilbestrol) exposure when in utero, originally used to help with miscarriages, used from '38 to '71. "DES syndrome" 
- clinical
    + presentation
        * abnormal bleeding (IBM, PCB, PMB)
        * abnormal discharge
        * dyspareunia
