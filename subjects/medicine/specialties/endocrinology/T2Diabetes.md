###### Type 2 diabetes


# Diagnostic criteria
- one of 3 tests can be used to diagnose T2DM in ASYMPTOMATIC
    + FBG
        * 7+ on 2 occasions
    + OGTT
        * 2 hr glucose level of 11.1 @75 g OGTT
    + HbA1c
        * HbA1c >6.5%
- OR in SYMPTOMATIC hyperglycaemia
    + sx: polyuria, thirst, weight loss, blurred vision
    + random blood glucose >11.1
