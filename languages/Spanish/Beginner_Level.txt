====== Beginner Level ======

===== Basic Phrases =====

Greetings
	Hola	hello, basic form, casual
	Chao		see you later, very informal
	Hasta luego 	See you later
	Hasta pronto 	see you soon (hasta luego more common)
	Nos vemos	we'll see eachother soon
	Que mas parce?  What's up br
	Que tal?	what's up, informal w/ friends
	Buenos dias 	formal good morning
	Buenas tardes	good afternoon
	Buenas noches/ Feliz noche	good evening
	Buenas		an abbreviation for buenas greetings
	Como estas? 	How are you, informal
	Como esta? 	Formal how are you?
	Como vas? 	How's it going
	Como va tu dia? How is your day?
	mucho gusto		nice to see you
	me encantada	nice to see you
	
Asking/ requesting/ accepting	
	Por favor		at the end of a request, eg pass the milk por favor, un beer por favor
	Gracias		thank you
	Lo siento		sorry
